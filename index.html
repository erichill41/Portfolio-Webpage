Hello! My name is Eric Hill.

I want to introduce myself so you and others can get to know more about me. Both on professional level, and to show some of what I like to do outside of working with code! On my webpage you'll find more information about me, a glance at some of the projects I have been working on, and several different ways to reach me.

Working in sales at Dell Technologies was a great opportunity for me to learn more about the technical side of the equipment we use. About Me

I started my career in sales after earning a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing from the University of Central Oklahoma. With a background in business and technology sales, I aim to bridge the gap of disconnect between technical teams and the business management side of things in small-medium businesses. My ability to problem solve, passion for efficiency, and working as a team are the reasons I feel confident in making this career change.

Outside of learning to develop software I'm a pretty active person, and living in Colorado certainly has its perks for being active! Some of my favorite things to do include: hockey, golf, general fitness, rock climbing, cycling, and exploring all that Colorado has to offer.
